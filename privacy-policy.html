Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mybtfecb ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and services, including our Facebook advertising tools.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Facebook / Meta data: When you connect your Facebook account, we access your ad account data, pages, and campaign information through the Meta Marketing API, solely to create and manage ad campaigns on your behalf.
  • Account information: Your name, email address, and Facebook user ID as provided by Meta during authentication.
  • Usage data: Logs of actions performed within the app (e.g., ad creation, campaign management) for troubleshooting and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected exclusively to:

  • Create, manage, and monitor Facebook ad campaigns on your behalf.
  • Authenticate your identity and authorize access to your Meta ad accounts.
  • Provide customer support and resolve technical issues.
  • Improve the functionality and performance of our service.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Facebook Permissions

Our application requests the following Meta / Facebook permissions:

  • ads_management — to create and manage ad campaigns.
  • ads_read — to read ad performance and reporting data.
  • pages_manage_ads — to manage ads associated with your Facebook Pages.
  • pages_read_engagement — to read page engagement data needed for ad creatives.
  • business_management — to access business assets linked to your ad accounts.

We only request the permissions necessary to provide the service. We do not use these permissions for any purpose other than what is described in this policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely on our servers and are never shared with unauthorized parties. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage and restricted access controls to protect your data.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — as required to operate through the Meta Marketing API.
  • Legal obligations — when required by applicable law or regulation.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Revoke the app's access to your Facebook account at any time via your Facebook App Settings.
