CORE LOGIC

One row = one product cluster

Workflow:

1. Find an initial ad from Ads Library.
2. Open the ad.
3. Visit or resolve the destination landing page URL.
4. Extract the REAL product page / landing page URL.
5. Extract product clues from that page:
   - page title
   - product title
   - H1/H2
   - repeated keywords
6. Use those extracted keywords to search again in Ads Library.
7. Find other advertisers selling the same product.
8. Build one product cluster.
9. Save one final grouped row.
