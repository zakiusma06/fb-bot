Create a new Telegram bot dedicated to pricing approved products.

This is a separate bot.

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL

The bot must review products from the APPROVED sheet tab that are not priced yet.

It should show products one by one and let me manually enter:

- PRICE
- COMPARE AT PRICE

Then it should update the same row in the APPROVED tab.

--------------------------------------------------
TARGET PRODUCTS

The bot must only show products from the APPROVED tab where pricing is not completed.

A product should be considered "not priced yet" if:
- PRICE is empty
OR
- COMPARE AT PRICE is empty

Only these products should be shown in the pricing bot.

--------------------------------------------------
COMMANDS

Add these commands:

/start
/price
/stats
/stop
/help

Also add clickable menu/buttons, not only typed commands.

--------------------------------------------------
MAIN FLOW

When I send /price:

1. Load all rows from the APPROVED tab
2. Filter only products not priced yet
3. Show them one by one

Message format example:

Product 3 / 42

Product page:
<URL PRODUCT>

Status:
APPROVED

Price:
<current PRICE or empty>

Compare at price:
<current COMPARE AT PRICE or empty>

Buttons:
- ADD PRICE
- SKIP
- STOP

--------------------------------------------------
ADD PRICE FLOW

When I click ADD PRICE:

Step 1:
Bot asks:
Enter PRICE

Step 2:
After I send PRICE, bot asks:
Enter COMPARE AT PRICE

Step 3:
After I send COMPARE AT PRICE:
- update the same row in the APPROVED tab
- save PRICE
- save COMPARE AT PRICE

Then reply:
✅ Pricing saved

Then automatically show the next not-yet-priced product.

--------------------------------------------------
SKIP BUTTON

When I click SKIP:
- do not change the row
- move to the next product

--------------------------------------------------
STOP BUTTON

When I click STOP or send /stop:
- stop the pricing session
- clear session state
- reply:
Pricing session stopped.

--------------------------------------------------
STATISTICS

Add pricing statistics similar to the previous bot.

Use either the existing STATISTICS tab or update it with a pricing section.

Track at least:

- APPROVED TOTAL
- PRICED
- NOT YET PRICED
- PRICING COMPLETION %

Definitions:
- APPROVED TOTAL = total rows in APPROVED
- PRICED = rows in APPROVED where PRICE and COMPARE AT PRICE are both filled
- NOT YET PRICED = APPROVED TOTAL - PRICED
- PRICING COMPLETION % = PRICED / APPROVED TOTAL * 100

When user sends /stats, show:

Pricing Statistics

Approved total: X
Priced: Y
Not yet priced: Z
Completion: XX%

Also update the STATISTICS sheet tab automatically.

--------------------------------------------------
GOOGLE SHEET UPDATE RULES

Do not change extraction bot.
Do not change approval bot.

This bot only updates rows in APPROVED.

It must preserve all existing columns.
It must only update:
- PRICE
- COMPARE AT PRICE

--------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT

- Keep all existing columns in the sheet
- Use the same Google Sheets file
- Keep code modular
- Support Telegram clickable menu/buttons
- Show products one by one
- Automatically continue after saving price
